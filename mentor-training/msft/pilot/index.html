Mentoring Toolkit

Resources for the Fall 2020 Pilot

The Mentoring Toolkit includes a Mentor Guide, Mentee Slides and a pack of videos.

Session 1: How do I craft my mentoring story?

July 14 from 10:30am - 12:30pm PT

What will you share with your mentees? One of the core activities you'll lead is around short videos that prompt reflection and sharing. How can you craft a relevant and meaningful story that will open up the space? This session will highlight the role of active listening and self-efficacy.

  • Session Page

Session 2: What if I don't know the answer?

July 17 from 10:30am - 12:30pm PT

How will you respond to questions from the students? We'll discuss the technical content you'll lead in the sessions and think about how to answer questions in the context of your role as a mentor in this program. This session will highlight the role of self-regulation and emotional intelligence.

  • Session Page

Session 3: How do I optimize the mentoring space?

July 21 from 10:30am - 12:30pm PT

As a mentor, you have the responsibility to cultivate an inclusive space where everyone feels respected and valued. What strategies will you use for facilitation that will enable all voices to be heard? This is particularly important in a remote setting. How can assumptions impact the interactions during a session? This session will highlight the role of belonging and effective feedback.

  • Session Page

Session 4: How do I facilitate a session?

July 24 from 10:30am - 12:30pm PT

You'll be leading sessions with another mentor. How will you work together to share different perspectives? What activities will help students step outside their comfort zones while still feeling supported? You'll practice leading components of the mentoring program and reflecting on your own comfort zones as a facilitator. This session will highlight the role of mindset and cognitive flexibility.

Self-Guided Work

4 half-hour activities to complete on your own

The scheduled sessions prioritize training components that lend themselves to group engagement. In this self-guided work, you will undertake 4 additional activities on your own; you should plan to spend about 30 minutes per activity, for a total of 2 hours.
