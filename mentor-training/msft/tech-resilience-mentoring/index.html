Microsoft Tech Resilience Mentoring

You'll be undertaking about 10 hours of preparation through synchronous sessions and self-guided activities. Mentor Resources

You’ll use a toolkit focused on building tech resilience for your mentees: the capability to persist and bounce back from setbacks, as well as be strategic in their journey. As a mentor, you will co-facilitate sessions for a cohort of mentees with the goal of helping them gain insights into and practice different components of tech resilience each session.

Be intentional (Do no harm)

Session 1

How can you be intentional in your mentoring? One of the core activities you'll lead is around short videos that prompt reflection and sharing to open up the space. Reflecting on your own tech journey will help you decide which stories on coping and overcoming struggle you are willing to share with mentees during the program. This session will highlight the role of active listening and self-efficacy.

  • Session Page

Optimize the mentoring space

Session 2

As a mentor, you have the responsibility to cultivate an inclusive space where everyone feels respected and valued. What strategies will you use for facilitation that will enable all voices to be heard? This is particularly important in a remote setting. How can assumptions impact the interactions during a session? This session will highlight the role of belongingness and effective feedback.

  • Session Page

Practice perspective-taking

Session 3

What can you do when something unexpected happens in the group setting? We can’t know everything that will come our way, but we can be prepared to pivot. Being flexible and willing to view the moment from different perspectives helps. Remember, you'll have a co-mentor who can help you steer; rely on each other. This session will highlight the role of cognitive flexibility and emotional intelligence.

Self-guided Modules

4 activities to complete on your own

The scheduled sessions prioritize training components that lend themselves to group engagement. In this self-guided work, you will undertake additional activities on your own; expected time varies between 45 minutes and an hour, for a total of about 3-4 hours.
