Session 1: Craft your mentoring story

January 29 from 12:30pm - 2:30pm PT

What will you share with your mentees? One of the core activities you'll lead is around short videos that prompt reflection and sharing. How can you craft a relevant and meaningful story that will open up the space? This session will highlight the role of active listening and self-efficacy.

Session 2: Open up the mentoring space

February 2 from 12:30pm - 2:30pm PT

As a mentor, you have the responsibility to cultivate an inclusive space where everyone feels respected and valued. What strategies will you use for facilitation that will enable all voices to be heard? This is particularly important in a remote setting. How can assumptions impact the interactions during a session? This session will highlight the role of belonging and effective feedback.

  • Session Page

Session 3: Steer the group dynamics

February 5 from 12:30pm - 2:30pm PT

What can you do when something unexpected happens in the group setting? Can you steer your cohort's dynamics to encourage an inclusive space and respectful interactions? This session will highlight the role of cognitive flexibility.

  • Session Page

Session 4: Strike a balance

February 9 from 12:30pm - 2:30pm PT

You'll be leading sessions with another mentor. How can you be strategic and work together to share different perspectives? How can you frame the activities to help students to connect the hands-on practice to resilience? This session will highlight the role of self-regulation.
