The Art Gallery Problem

Click/drag inside the (white) region to place/move guards. Unguarded areas are white, so you have succeeded if the interior is completely green. Overlapping areas of coverage are a deeper shade of green.

Help

  • You can move a guard by dragging within the polygon.
  • You can remove a guard by clicking it or dragging it outside of the polygon.
  • You can add a new guard by clicking anywhere inside the polygon.
  • You can move a polygon point by dragging it. Be careful to not cause the polygon to intersect with itself!
  • You can remove a polygon point by clicking it.
  • You can add a new polygon point by clicking anywhere outside the polygon.

    • Adapted from the projects of Daniel Johnson and Michelle Aniuchi.
